The story

Behind
the lens

[ dylan ]

"The HD mark comes from my family's cattle brand — burned into the land for generations, and now on everything I create."

Dylan Watt is a photographer and creative director based in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. He grew up on the prairies, spent years managing territory sales across agricultural Western Canada, and somewhere along the way realized that what he actually wanted to do was make things.

After his role in ag sales ended in early 2026, he spent two months backpacking through Southeast Asia — camera in hand, no fixed plan. He came home with a clearer eye, a hard drive full of images, and a determination to build something of his own.

HD Creative came out of that. A small studio built around honest visual work — photography, video, and web design for people and businesses who care about how they're seen. The name comes from his family's cattle brand. The work comes from years of watching what actually moves people.

Dylan works with individuals who want images that feel real, and brands that want creative that does more than just look good.
